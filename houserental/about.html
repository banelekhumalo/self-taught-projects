Growing up was very awesome, knowing very well that your environment and surroundings individuals are positive, goal driven beings and the whole kasi raised us, like they always say "It takes the whole village to raise a child". I never thought being a programmer would be something that will put bread on top of the table, but hey look at us pressing keybords, waisting data for a good use.

This is my playground, a platform for me to explore things and expand my skills. I am still learnig and I would also like you to be part of it. Are you having ideas but you do not know where to turn? I am here, let's uplift each other.
